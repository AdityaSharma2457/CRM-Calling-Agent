Apex University Admissions Information

Apex University offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Our admissions process is streamlined, and we offer competitive fees and robust academic support.

Courses Offered & Fee Structure:
1. Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Computer Science: Duration is 4 years. The tuition fee is $15,000 per year.
2. Master of Business Administration (MBA): Duration is 2 years. The tuition fee is $20,000 per year.
3. Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) in Data Science: Duration is 3 years. The tuition fee is $12,000 per year.
4. Master of Science (M.Sc) in Artificial Intelligence: Duration is 2 years. The tuition fee is $18,000 per year.

Important Admission Dates & Deadlines:
- Fall Semester Application Deadline: July 31, 2026.
- Fall Semester Classes Start: September 5, 2026.
- Spring Semester Application Deadline: November 30, 2026.
- Spring Semester Classes Start: January 15, 2027.

Admission Requirements:
- Undergraduate Programs: Minimum 60% in High School (Class 12th) examinations. English proficiency test scores (IELTS minimum 6.0 or TOEFL minimum 80) for international students.
- Postgraduate Programs: Bachelor's degree in a relevant field with a minimum GPA of 3.0 out of 4.0. Prior work experience is preferred but not mandatory for MBA programs.

Scholarships & Financial Aid:
- Academic Merit Scholarship: Up to 50% tuition fee waiver for top 5% of enrolled students.
- Need-based Financial Aid: Up to 30% tuition fee waiver based on family income criteria.
